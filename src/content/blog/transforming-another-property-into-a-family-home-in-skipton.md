At Wilkins Naylor, our role goes beyond lettings and property management. We are actively involved in creating high quality homes for the private rental market.

We are currently undertaking a new renovation project in Skipton, where a previously unlivable and unmortgageable property is being transformed into a modern family home. Properties in this condition are often left vacant or overlooked, but we focus on identifying their underlying potential.

This project involves a full refurbishment, including structural works, modernisation, and a complete internal upgrade. The aim is to deliver a durable, well designed home that meets the expectations of today’s tenants while providing long term reliability.

Once complete, the property will be brought into the private rental market and professionally managed by Wilkins Naylor. This ensures not only a high standard of finish but also a consistent level of service for tenants.

By targeting properties that are currently unfit for habitation or difficult to finance, we are able to bring homes back into use that might otherwise remain empty. This approach supports local housing supply while maintaining the standards we expect across our portfolio.

We will continue to share updates as the project progresses and look forward to adding another high quality home in Skipton to our managed portfolio.
