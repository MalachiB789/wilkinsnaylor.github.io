## Main heading

Write your next section here. Leave a blank line between paragraphs.

### Smaller heading

Use `##` for bigger section headings and `###` for smaller headings.

Use `[[br]]` inside a paragraph if you want to force a new line without starting a new paragraph.

- Use dashes for bullet points
- Keep each point on its own line

You can also add **bold text**, *italic text*, and [links](https://example.com).
