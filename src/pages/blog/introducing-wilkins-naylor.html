Website update: introducing Wilkins Naylor

17 February 2026

We have launched a new website bringing Wilkins Naylor Ltd and Naylor Homes Ltd together under a single brand, Wilkins Naylor.

We have launched a new website bringing Wilkins Naylor Ltd and Naylor Homes Ltd together under a single brand, Wilkins Naylor.

Over time the business has grown to include both family homes and a small number of professionally managed shared houses. Operating under separate company names made sense administratively, but it was not always clear to tenants which part of the business they were dealing with. The new website simplifies this. Everything now sits in one place, with clearer information about the properties we offer, how we manage them, and what tenants can expect.

What has changed

The new site provides a clearer structure and reflects how the business actually operates. Rather than separate identities, Wilkins Naylor is now presented as one rental business with two types of accommodation.

Family homes
Modernised houses intended for working professionals and families seeking a stable, long term home.

Shared homes
A small number of low occupancy shared houses with large en suite rooms, business grade broadband, and professionally cleaned communal areas.

We have also expanded the information available to tenants. The website now explains our approach to maintenance, communication, deposits, and tenancy expectations. The aim is to answer common questions before a viewing is even arranged.

Why we made the change

Renting a home is a significant commitment. Many tenants want to know who they are renting from, how issues will be handled, and whether they can expect stability. Previously, that information was spread across different pages and company names. Bringing everything together under Wilkins Naylor makes accountability clearer and avoids unnecessary confusion.

The new site also allows us to provide practical guidance. We will be using the blog to publish straightforward updates on property standards, maintenance, and the rental process so tenants understand how we operate and what they can expect throughout a tenancy.

What stays the same

Although the branding has changed, the way we operate has not. Properties remain professionally managed, communication is handled directly, and the focus continues to be long term tenancies rather than short term lets. We still do not let to students and we do not operate holiday accommodation.

Our objective remains simple: well maintained homes, clear communication, and a predictable renting experience.

If you are looking for a property or want to understand how we work, the new website provides a clearer overview. We hope it makes the process more straightforward for both existing and prospective tenants.
