About Wilkins Naylor

Wilkins Naylor is a privately owned rental business operating across Yorkshire and Lancashire, comprising Wilkins Naylor Ltd and Naylor Homes Ltd.

What We Do

We manage a growing portfolio of family homes, professional lets and high-specification HMOs, all run to a consistent standard. While we operate at scale, our approach remains hands-on. We believe tenants should know who they are renting from and how their home will be looked after. We focus on straightforward communication, reliable management, and homes that are maintained consistently over the long term. Our priority is stable, high-quality living for people who want certainty and professionalism from their landlord.

What we specialise in

Our core lettings focus is clear and intentional.

Homes for families

Well-maintained houses designed for long-term tenants who value reliable standards and responsive management.

High-end shared houses

High-specification HMOs with en-suite double rooms, created for professionals seeking quality shared living, with business broadband and professional cleaning included.

Long-term tenancy approach

We do not let to students or operate short-term accommodation. Our service is built around stable, long-term homes that are maintained to a high standard.

Features of our homes and tenancies

  • Professionally maintained to a consistent standard
  • Minimum EPC rating of C to help reduce energy costs
  • Good levels of insulation for comfort and efficiency
  • Modern kitchens and appliances
  • Up to date gas safety and electrical safety certification in place
  • Reliable heating and hot water systems
  • Mains pressure showers
  • Prompt response to maintenance issues – 24-48h call outs
  • Clear and straightforward tenancy agreements
  • Fair and transparent deposit handling
  • Long-term, stable tenancies with no unnecessary disruption
