Bringing More Homes Back into Use for the Skipton Community

22 March 2026

Wilkins Naylor brings neglected and empty properties back into use, transforming them into high quality homes for renters in Skipton and the surrounding areas.

At Wilkins Naylor, our focus is on more than just managing properties. We are committed to bringing homes back into use and making a positive contribution to the local community.

Across Skipton and the surrounding areas, there are properties that have fallen into disrepair or are no longer suitable for modern living. In many cases, these homes sit empty because they are unmortgageable or require significant work to make them livable again.

We take these properties and give them a second life.

By carrying out full renovations, we are able to turn neglected or unused buildings into safe, comfortable homes for people and families. Each property is carefully improved to ensure it meets a high standard, both in terms of quality and practicality.

Once complete, these homes are brought back into the rental market and managed by Wilkins Naylor. This means tenants benefit from well maintained properties and a reliable, responsive service.

This approach not only creates more housing for renters, but also helps improve the overall condition of the local area. Empty or run down properties can have a negative impact on neighbourhoods, so bringing them back into use benefits everyone.

Our aim is simple. To take homes that are no longer fit for purpose and turn them into places people are proud to live in, while supporting the wider community in Skipton.
