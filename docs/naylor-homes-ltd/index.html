Naylor Homes Ltd

Naylor Homes Ltd is one of the limited companies within our group. If you rent through this part of the business, your tenancy agreement and formal documentation will be issued in this company name.

Our service

Naylor Homes Ltd offers a mix of high quality living accommodation for families together with a small number of high end shared houses. These HMOs are deliberately low occupancy, typically providing large double bedrooms with an en suite in every room.

Each tenancy includes business grade broadband and professionally cleaned shared areas on a weekly basis. Our aim is to provide a high standard of accommodation and management at a fair and reasonable price. We do not operate short term or low quality lets. The focus is stable accommodation, normally with a minimum tenancy of six months. In certain circumstances we may consider three month tenancies on a case by case basis.
